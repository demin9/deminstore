About Demin Store

Demin Store is an online shopping website that connects customers with multiple vendors. Our mission is to provide a seamless and enjoyable shopping experience for our customers while also supporting small and independent businesses.

Our Story

Demin Store was founded in 2023 by Mahsa. As a passionate shopper and supporter of small businesses, Mahsa saw an opportunity to create an online marketplace that would bring together a wide range of vendors and offer customers a diverse selection of products.

Since then, Demin Store has grown to become one of the leading online shopping websites with multiple vendors, serving customers across the world.

Our Values

  • Customer satisfaction: We strive to provide the best possible shopping experience for our customers and are committed to their satisfaction.
  • Diversity and inclusivity: We believe in offering a diverse selection of products from different vendors and promoting inclusivity in all aspects of our business.
  • Sustainability: We are committed to reducing our environmental impact and promoting sustainable practices among our vendors and customers.
  • Community: We value the relationships we have with our vendors and customers and are committed to building a strong and supportive community.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, comments, or feedback, please don't hesitate to contact us. You can reach us through our online form or our social media channels.
